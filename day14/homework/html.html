"Shadow doxing" is not a widely recognized term. It's likely a misunderstanding or a conflation of two distinct concepts: shadow boxing and doxing. Shadow boxing is a training exercise in boxing where a fighter practices punches and movements without an opponent. Doxing, on the other hand, is the act of researching and publicly revealing private information about someone.
